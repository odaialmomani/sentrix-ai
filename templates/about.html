Attack Reference
DoS Attack Critical
Floods CAN Bus with 0x000 messages at maximum rate, overwhelming all ECUs and halting vehicle communication.
IPS Response
Blocks 0x000 messages instantly — restores normal CAN Bus traffic.
Fuzzy Attack High
Injects random CAN messages with arbitrary IDs, confusing ECUs and causing unpredictable vehicle behavior.
IPS Response
Filters invalid CAN IDs — validates all messages before ECU delivery.
